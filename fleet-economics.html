What to weigh up

The honest checklist before switching
  • Your daily distance. Under about 50 km/day the fuel saving may not cover the flat subscription. This product is for working riders.
  • Swap coverage on your routes. The economics assume a swap station within your working area. Check coverage for your district before committing.
  • Resale value. The second hand market for electric bikes in Vietnam is young. Gas bikes hold value predictably today; treat EV resale as unproven for now.
  • Subscription terms. ₫1,450,000/month reflects current partner pricing. Like any service, terms can change at renewal; the contract, not the brochure, governs.
